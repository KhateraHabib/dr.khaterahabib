Who is Dr. Khatera Habib Wali?

Dr. Khatera Habib Wali (داکتر خاطره حبیب ولی) is one of Afghanistan's leading dermatologists, combining academic excellence with clinical expertise. She serves as Assistant Professor at Kabul Medical University and is a specialist in Dermatology and Venereology at the Department of Dermatology, Maiwand Teaching Hospital.

With over 10 years of clinical experience and 15+ international research publications in prestigious databases including PubMed, Wiley (International Journal of Dermatology), and Springer Nature, Dr. Khatera Habib Wali represents the highest standard of dermatological care in Afghanistan.
